# Limitations and Ethics

- All companies, customers, subscriptions, payments, and outcomes are synthetic.
- Generated patterns do not establish real-world causality.
- Retention models should avoid discriminatory use of protected characteristics.
- Discounts can reduce churn while harming long-term unit economics.
- Customer-level interventions require privacy, consent, access-control, and
  communication-frequency review.
- Production churn decisions should include fairness, model drift, false-positive,
  and customer-impact monitoring.
- Confidential employer or customer data should not be entered into public AI
  systems without explicit authorization and appropriate safeguards.
