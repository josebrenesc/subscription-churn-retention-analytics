# Business Case

## Scenario

Helio Subscription Services is a **fictional** subscription company offering
Basic, Plus, Premium, and Business plans.

Leadership needs a consolidated view of churn, retention, recurring revenue,
payment failures, refunds, product usage, cancellation reasons, save offers,
reactivations, and customer experience.

## Business questions

1. How is churn changing over time?
2. Which plans and customer segments have the weakest retention?
3. Which cancellation reasons create the greatest loss of subscriptions?
4. How are usage, payment issues, and support experience associated with churn?
5. How much current MRR and ARR is generated by active subscriptions?
6. Which acquisition channels produce customers with stronger retention?
7. How effective are save offers and reactivation channels?
8. How does cohort retention develop through month 12?

## Deliverables

- Raw and cleaned relational datasets
- SQLite analytical database
- SQL analysis
- Python cleaning and analysis workflow
- Excel executive dashboard
- Power BI model and DAX measures
- Cohort retention analysis
- Executive findings and recommendations

## Scope

This portfolio case study uses synthetic data. It demonstrates a subscription
analytics workflow and does not represent a real employer or production system.
